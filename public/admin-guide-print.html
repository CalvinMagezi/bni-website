Section 1

Signing In

1
Go to the admin URL
Open your browser and visit: boysnetworkinternational.com/admin/login
2
Click "Continue with Google"
A Google sign-in window will appear. This uses your existing Google account — no separate password needed.
3
Choose your authorised account
Only email addresses on the BNI allowed list can access the admin panel. If you see an "Access Denied" message after signing in, your email is not on the list — contact the site administrator.
4
You're in!
You'll be taken to the Dashboard automatically. Use the left-hand sidebar to navigate between all sections.
📊
Section 2

Dashboard

1
Overview cards
The dashboard shows at a glance: total camp posts, stories, contact messages, newsletter subscribers, and enrollment requests.
2
+ New Camp Post
Click the blue "New Camp Post" card to jump straight to creating a post for the Camp Live feed.
3
View Inbox
Click "View Inbox" to see all messages from parents and visitors who have filled in forms on the website.
4
Left sidebar navigation
The sidebar is always visible. Click any item to jump to that section. Your name and email appear at the top so you always know which account you're signed in with.
📝
Section 3

Camp Posts

1
What are posts?
Posts appear on the Camp Live page as the main social feed — like a WhatsApp status or Instagram post. Parents see these updates to follow along with the bootcamp in real time.
2
Creating a new post
Click "+ New Post" (top right of the posts list). Fill in: Author name, their Role, and the post text. Optionally attach a photo URL. Set "Is Live?" to No while drafting, then Yes when ready to publish.
3
Draft vs Published
Live = No keeps the post hidden from visitors. Live = Yes makes it visible on the website. You can always switch back to No to hide it again.
4
Ordering posts
The "Position" number controls the order. 0 = top of the feed, 1 = second, and so on. Give your most recent post the lowest number.
5
Editing a post
Click "Edit" on any post in the list to change the text, photo, time label, or live status.
6
Deleting a post
Click "Delete" to permanently remove a post. This cannot be undone — if unsure, set Live = No to hide it instead.
🎬
Section 4

Stories

1
What are stories?
Stories are the circular photo bubbles at the top of the Camp Live page — similar to Instagram Stories. Each one highlights a participant or a camp moment.
2
Adding a story
Scroll to the "Add Story" form at the bottom of the page. Enter: a Label (person's name, e.g. "Bryan M."), an Image URL (direct link to their photo), and whether it should be Live.
3
Live status
A story with Live = Yes shows a red "LIVE" badge on the Camp Live page. Use this during active camp sessions to highlight who is currently featured.
4
Deleting a story
Click "Delete" next to any story to remove it from the page immediately.
📈
Section 5

Camp Stats

1
What are camp stats?
The 4 key numbers shown in the Camp Live sidebar — for example: "47 Boys Enrolled", "Day 3 of Camp", "3 Mentors". These appear live on the website.
2
Editing stats
Click directly into any field and type your change. You can update: the emoji icon, the label text (e.g. "Boys Enrolled"), and the value (e.g. "47").
3
Saving changes
Click "Save All Stats" at the bottom. All 4 stats are saved at once and go live on the website immediately.
🖼️
Section 6

Gallery

1
Albums
The gallery is organised into albums — for example "Rise & Thrive 2024" or "Leadership Camp 2023". Each album has a cover photo and a collection of photos inside it.
2
Creating an album
Click "+ New Album". Enter: a Title (e.g. "Rise & Thrive 2024"), a Slug (URL name with no spaces, e.g. rise-thrive-2024), and a Cover Image URL.
3
Adding photos
Click "Manage Photos" on any album. Use the Add Photo form at the bottom — paste the image URL and a short description of the photo. Click "Add Photo".
4
Deleting a photo
Click "Delete" next to any photo. The photo count in the album updates automatically.
5
Deleting an album
Click "Delete" on an album card from the Gallery list. This removes the album and its public page from the website.
👥
Section 7

Team Members

1
What is this?
Team members appear in the "Meet the Founders" section on the About page. This is how visitors learn about the BNI leadership team.
2
Adding a member
Fill in: their Name, Title/role (e.g. "Co-Founder & Director"), and optionally paste a headshot image URL. Set an Order number — lower appears first (0 = first card).
3
Removing a member
Click "Remove" on their card. They will no longer appear on the public About page.
📬
Section 8

Inbox

1
Read-only
The Inbox shows all form submissions from the public website. You can view but not delete entries from here.
2
Contact Messages
Submitted via the Contact page. Shows the date, name, email, phone number, and message. Click any email address to open a reply in your email app.
3
Newsletter Subscribers
Email addresses collected from the newsletter signup on the website, with the date they signed up.
4
Enrollment Intakes
Submitted via the Camp Live enrollment chatbot. Shows the child's name, age, area of interest, and the parent's email address. Click the email to follow up.

💡 Helpful Tips

Image URLs
The site uses image URLs instead of file uploads. To use a photo, upload it to Google Drive with public sharing and copy the direct link — or use any hosted image link.
Drafts vs Live
Always create posts as "Live = No" first, check they look right, then switch to "Live = Yes" to publish. You can always switch back to hide them.
Ordering content
The Position number controls what appears first. Position 0 = top of the page. You can use any numbers you like — 0, 1, 2, 3… or 10, 20, 30 if you want gaps to insert new items later.
Stats are instant
Camp Stats appear on the website as soon as you click "Save All Stats". Posts and stories only appear after you set them to Live = Yes.
Deletions are permanent
Once you delete a post, story, photo, or team member it cannot be undone from the admin panel. If unsure, hide it with Live = No instead of deleting.
Signing out
Click "Sign out" at the bottom of the left sidebar to end your session securely.
